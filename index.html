CS 488 Project
Ray tracer
1. Primitives
Support Cylinder and Cone as new primitive.
2. CSG
CSG is used to construct complex shapes. Operation includes "union", "intersection", and "difference"
3. Reflection
Mirror reflection on surface.
4. Refraction
The ray passing through a translucent/transparent should obey Snell's Law.
5. Texture Mapping
Use texture mapping to make the scene looks better.
6. Bump Mapping
Apply bump mapping so that object looks more realistic.
7. Soft Shadow
Multiple shadow ray is casted to create a soft shadow.
8. Adaptive Anti-aliasing
The picture in low resolution will look better when applying Adaptive Anti-aliasing technique.
9. Motion Blur
Moving objects are blurred to reflect the moving speed.
10. Final Scene
A unique final scene to demonstrate the outcome of this project and the course.
